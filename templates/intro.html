What is Federated Learning ?


Federated Learning is simply the decentralized form of Machine Learning.
In Machine Learning, we usually train our data that is aggregated from several edge devices like mobiles, laptops, etc. and is brought to a centralized server. In Federated Learning, we enable these devices to collaboratively learn shared prediction model while keeping all training data on device.
